FOR ALL THE WORLD’S GOLFERS

The World Golf Competition is a true open golf event drawing players from across the globe. Any golfer can accept the challenge and become the world champion. All golfers can compete, male, female, amateurs and professionals. The final winner is the world golf champion and will have won their way through a unique format played on some of the world’s best championship courses.

HOW IT WORKS

The World Golf Competition encompasses five rounds of championship golf. Winners progress through round one and two in their nominated zones. Round two winners progress to the nine regional finals, played at exclusive resort courses across the globe. Winners that progress through the regional finals will be flown to one of three exclusive zone finals in Europe, Asia Pacific or America. 216 Winners of the zone finals will be invited to play in the World Golf Competition - Open Final. Played over two days at an exclusive 5-Star Resort Championship Course in the Caribbean. The World Final will culminate with a Grand Gala Dinner, presenting the winner and runners up with their respective final trophies and prizes.
